Ben Fry and Casey Reas are the co-founders of Processing and the primary designers of the language and software.

The team leading the current push forward to Processing 1.5 includes:
Andres Colubri, new video library, OpenGL integration
Damien Di Fede, core sound library
Ira Greenberg, master of ceremonies
Florian Jenett, design director and net burgermeister
Eric Jordan, graphics weapon
Chandler McWilliams, tools development
Andreas Schlegel, library director
Dan Shiffman, man about town and library consultant

The core Processing software is augmented by libraries contributed through the community. Inventive libraries are the bright future of this project. A list of developers and their libraries is maintained here. This contribution can't be underestimated.

We're always looking for people to join in. We hope to distribute the development of the project among a small group of dedicated individuals. The current state of Processing would not be possible without shared effort. The Processing software is a community effort. It has evolved through a continuous conversation.

We give a special "Thank You!" to Sami Arola for writing the base of P3D, Simon Greenwold for incorporting camera and lights into P3D, Florian Jenett for years of work on the website, and Tom Carden for creating Processing Hacks and maintaining Processing Blogs.
